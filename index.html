This red-smudged rune means "Hunter", and has been adopted by those who have taken the Hunter of Hunters oath.

Radiance
A secret symbol left by Caryll, runesmith of Byrgenwerth.The rune for "Radiance," adopted by the sworn Executioners under Logarius' command.
Embrace
After the repeated experiments in controlling the scourge of beasts, the gentle "Embrace" rune was discovered.
Milkweed
A Caryll rune envisioned by Adeline, patient of the Research Hall.A translation of the inhuman, sticky whispers that reveal the nature of a celestial attendant.
Impurity
A Caryll rune that transcribes inhuman sounds. This rune, discovered inside the forbidden beast eater, came to symbolize "Impurity," and the oath of The League.
